About St. Joseph College Inc.

Vision

St. Joseph College - Olongapo Inc. envisions a Christ-centered, dynamic, and effective academic community that adheres to the Catholic philosophy of education and national development goals.

Mission

To prepare self-directed, responsible, humane, God-loving, and environment-caring citizens and leaders who are academically and vocationally equipped to cope with life and to be ready to serve others.

Goals

St. Joseph College - Olongapo Inc. implements an educational program aimed to produce responsible, competent and productive individuals fired with Christian values.

History

St. Joseph College Olongapo (often abbreviated as "SJCO") is a Catholic institution dedicated to spreading wisdom and providing education to students in Olongapo, including the neighboring towns of Zambales and Bataan.

It is the oldest parochial school in Olongapo City and Central Luzon, founded by Father Martin Moran, an Irish diocesan priest.

St. Joseph College Olongapo Inc. was officially established after World War II by the Society of the Divine Word Fathers in 1945. Fr. Rizalino Veneracion served as the first director.
